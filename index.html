Fresh Herbed Chicken Breasts

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
boneless chicken breasts with skin on 12 oz
garlic, pressed 2 medium cloves
fresh squeezed lemon juice n 1 TBS
chopped fresh sage 2 tsp
chopped fresh thyme 2 tsp
chopped fresh rosemary 1 tsp
chicken broth 1/4 cup
salt and cracked black pepper 1 tsp

Step 1: eheat broiler on high. Place ovenproof metal pan under broiler to get hot. Do not use glass or Pyrex for this.

Step 2: Season chicken with a little salt and pepper. When pan is very hot (which takes about 5 minutes), put chicken in pan and return it to broiler. Turn heat to low. Don't put it too close to the heating element. It is best to put in middle of the oven, about 7 inches from the heat source. (Quick Broil) for about 15 minutes, or until done, depending on thickness of chicken.

Step 3: While chicken is cooking chop herbs.

Step 4: In a small skillet, add chopped herbs, lemon juice, broth, pressed garlic, salt, and pepper. Heat on medium heat for about 30 seconds.

Step 5: When chicken is done remove skin, slice, and place on platter. Drizzle herb sauce over chicken.

Spice Chicken in a Bowl

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
boneless, skinless chicken breasts 2
chicken broth g4 cups
sliced fresh ginger, sliced (or 1/2 tsp dried ginger) 1/2 inch
Garlic 6 whole cloves
chopped fresh thyme 2 tsp
cinnamon sticks 2
anise 5 star
dried fennel seeds r 1/2 tsp
onion, chopped in big pieces 1 medium-sized
dried shiitake mushrooms 6 whole medium
dried fennel seeds 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Chop onion and garlic and let sit for 5 minutes to bring out their hidden health-promoting benefits.

Step 2: Healthy Sauté onion in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Add garlic and continue to sauté for another minute. Add chicken broth, ginger, cloves, cinnamon stick, star anise, fennel seeds, and shiitake mushrooms. Turn heat to medium and simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 3: While broth is simmering, cut ends off beans and cut into 1-inch pieces.While broth is simmering, cut ends off beans and cut into 1-inch pieces.

Step 4: Strain broth mixture (saving shiitake mushrooms) and put liquid back into pan. Bring to a boil, add chicken pieces and green beans, and cook on medium heat for 7-10 minutes. You may need to skim the surface a little while chicken pieces are cooking. Slice shiitake mushrooms and add to chicken and broth. Add scallion as well as salt and pepper to taste.

Curried Chicken Over Spinach

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into bite sized pieces 3
chicken stock 1 1/2 cup
garlic, sliced 3 cloves
fresh ginger chopped, or 1/2 tsp dried 1 TBS
turmeric 1/2 tsp
curry powder 1 tsp
onion, cut in half and sliced 1 medium sized
red bell pepper julienne about 1 inch long r 1 medium sized
coconut milk, make sure it is mixed well before using 1/2 cup
fresh spinach 4 bunches
salt & white pepper to taste 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Bring water to a boil for spinach. While water is coming to a boil, cut chicken into bite sized pieces. Healthy Sauté onion in a medium sauté pan over medium low heat for about 5 minutes stirring frequently. Add garlic and fresh ginger and continue to sauté for another minute. Add turmeric, and curry and mix well. Add stock, chicken, and coconut milk. Simmer for 5 minutes and add bell peppers and other vegetables you desire. Cook until chicken is done, about another 5 minutes.

Step 2: While chicken is cooking, cut ends off the bunch of spinach all at once. Don't bother trying to do it one stem at a time. It will take you too long and it is not necessary. Rinse spinach well and drop into boiling water for just 1 minute. Strain and press dry. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Place spinach on plates and top with chicken mixture.

Source: Curried Chicken Over Spinach
